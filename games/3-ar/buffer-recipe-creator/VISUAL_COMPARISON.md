## Interaction Comparison

### OLD Game Loop:
1. Read problem
2. Open calculator
3. Do math on paper
4. Type numbers
5. Click submit
6. Hope for green checkmark
7. **Time spent:** 3-5 minutes

2. Add/remove molecules
